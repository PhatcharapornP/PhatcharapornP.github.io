Fantasy RPG Snake

A snake game with an RPG twist.

Introduction

A snake game reimagined with RPG mechanics: your ‘snake’ is a growing party of heroes that battles enemies as it moves. There's no win state — you play for as long as you like.

Role
Solo
Duration
6 days (4–6hr/day)
Engine
Unity
GitHub ↗ Back
01

Gameplay

  • Collect heroes as you move and use them to battle enemies along the way.
  • Switch the party leader or rotate between members of the party.
  • Adjustable 2D tile-map size.
  • Tweakable spawn rates — heroes, enemies, and obstacles each have their own spawn percentage.
  • Every move increases the stats of all heroes and enemies.
02

Systems

  • A state machine and a pool system for the board, plus a tweakable data container adjusted at runtime — spawn percentages tuned so the player can always move freely.
  • Every unit implements a shared interface for movement, stats, and collision callbacks — the party leader colliding with a unit either collects a hero or enters a battle with an enemy.
  • Lists, dictionaries, and queues track all units and their positions to populate the board.
