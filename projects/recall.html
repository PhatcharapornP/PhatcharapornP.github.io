Recall

The flagship AR content viewer for Illusion Connect Thailand — scan a marker, review videos, 3D models, and animations in AR, then share straight to social media.

Introduction

I joined after Recall had already been running for a while, but it had become hard to maintain — let alone update or add features to. That led me to rework the whole application and re-organize how its assets were set up. What follows are the systems that came out of it.
