Centipede Game

A shooting game — clear the entire centipede for the highest score.

Introduction

A small solo exercise; the interesting bit is the centipede's behaviour when you shoot different parts of it. Object pooling for bullets and dictionaries for positions kept it light enough to finish with ease.

Role
Solo
Duration
6 days (~4hr/day)
Engine
Unity
GitLab ↗ Back
01

Gameplay

  • Shoot to destroy every centipede to win and chase the highest score.
  • Shoot the leader's head and the next segment becomes the new head, leading the centipede forward.
  • Shoot the middle and the trailing half breaks off as a new, independent centipede — repeating until none are left.
  • Destroy obstacles freely — the player can take them out whenever they want.
02

Systems

  • Object pooling for bullets.
  • Dictionaries for tracking centipede, obstacle, and player positions.
