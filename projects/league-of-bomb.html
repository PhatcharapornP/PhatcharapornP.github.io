League of Bomb — Nanuq Studio

Built core combat systems on a deterministic multiplayer simulation for a real-time mobile game (closed beta) — a reusable ability framework behind 10+ character skills/ultimates; a VFX/SFX pipeline driving skills, animations, and projectiles across the Photon Quantum simulation ↔ Unity view boundary, keeping the two in sync and handling cancellations; a unified status-effect system for power-ups and debuffs; and an aiming/targeting system for skill-shots. Also delivered live-service features on the meta side: social (friends/chat/mailbox), progression/economy (gacha, achievements, quests), and the loadout system connecting menu choices to in-match abilities.
