Airship: Kingdoms Adrift

An airship trading and combat simulator set in a steampunk world. Shipped on Steam, GOG, and the Epic Games Store with Revolution Industry's team.

Introduction

Airship was one of the most challenging projects I've been part of — and my first published game. I was lucky to learn from the senior programmers overseeing it and to work alongside a talented team, and it pushed me into a lot of things I'd never built before. The work below is that new territory.
