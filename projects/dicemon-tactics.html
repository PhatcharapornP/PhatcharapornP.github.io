Dicemon Tactics — Nanuq Studio

Client-side gameplay and live-service systems for Dicemon Tactics, Nanuq Studio's “Dicemon” gacha monster-collector for mobile — new Dicemon skills built on a node-graph system, the gacha shop and summoning cutscene, the progression loop (level-up, transmute, star promotion), and the meta layer (realtime chat, season pass, mail, and animated post-match chest rewards).

01

Gameplay & progression

  • Dicemon skills — authored new skills on a node-graph skill system, extending it with new graph nodes where a skill needed behaviour the existing nodes couldn't express.
  • Summoning cutscene — the pull-opening sequence built as a state machine paired with Unity Timeline, with rarity-based VFX and first-time-user variants.
  • Gacha shop — banners with single and 10× pulls, ticket currency, drop-rate display, and SSR pity.
  • Dicemon level-up — feeding XP materials to cross per-rarity thresholds and raise stats.
  • Transmute — converting surplus units, chips, and costumes into the upgrade materials the other flows consume.
  • Star promotion — ascending a Dicemon by sacrificing duplicates for per-star skill effects.
02

Live-service & social

  • Chat — multi-channel realtime chat (channel, direct, club, and announcements) over a WebSocket backend.
  • Post-match chest rewards — an animated chest-opening flow (state machine + Unity Timeline) with rarity upgrades and gating.
  • Season pass — free and premium tracks, XP-driven levels, reward claims, and paid level skips.
  • Mail — a server-driven inbox with system and gift mail, attachments, and claim-all.
